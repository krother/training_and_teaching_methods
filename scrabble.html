Scrabble

Summary
Recap some keywords.
Preparation time
none
Group size
3-30
What you need
whiteboard or flipchart

How it is done

Write one keyword in the middle of the board. Ask the group to suggest other keywords in a crossword-like manner.

The exercise is not to be taken too seriously. You don't need to enforce rules of normal corsswords meticulously. You also don't need to stick precisely to a grid when drawing characters.

As an extra incentive, you may add some bonus fields along the edges.
