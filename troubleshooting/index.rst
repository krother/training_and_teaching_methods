
Troubleshooting
===============

No matter how well you prepare, there will always be situations that disrupt your plan.
Exceptional situations are inevitable, but they do not have to crash your lesson.
Like a well-built software has a way to deal with its own errors,
you should have your own set of emergency procedures.
The following chapters describe a few such emergency procedures for the most common situations.
